Course Project FAQ

Here are a number of frequently asked questions regarding the course project:

How can I check the weight of a web page?
Navigate to the page you want to check, then click the YSlow tool (installed in Chrome as part of the Bookmark Class resources exercise). Click the "Run Test" button. Check out the components page for the weight of the page.
What are the usability concepts to include within the proposal?
Usability concepts are those things that make our page content more friendly and useful to a site visitor. We will discuss specific items listed in the Usability Checklist, but additional usability items are discussed in the readings from the Nelson / Norman website that appear in the weekly overview pages.
What are the video options at the bottom of the project description page?
As part of the course project you have to create two videos, 1) a content video that you will place in the one of the content pages using the HTML5 video element, and 2) a video where you demonstrate the construction and use of your project web site that will be placed in the homework page. The links are there as resources, if you need them, to produce these videos.
Can I use Vimeo or YouTube to house my video and link to the video from my web site?
The content video should be hosted on your own website and inserted into the content page using the HTML5 video element. The site construction and demonstration video could be hosted on an external service and then embedded into your site.
